# Listicle Format

- Headline: Reference number of products and theme 
  - E.g. "12 activewear brands for your workout wardrobe"
- Lede (optional): 100-300 words providing context
- Product Entry (repeated for each product):
  - Headline: Product name
  - Image: Promotional, user-generated, or custom
  - Description: 20-40 word blurb 
  - Review: 50-200 word review/endorsement
  - Call-to-Action: Price and affiliate link

# General Guidelines
- Round up many products under a common theme
- Don't deeply review/endorse each product
- Gear towards personal consumption, not gifting
- May omit lede and conclusion
- Use affiliate links and calls-to-action
- Can use breakout boxes for images/links
